About me!

I like everything to do with knowledge and intellect. I also strongly believe in peace and compassion. My biggest wish is a world without war where people respect the fact that others can have different opinions and beliefs. A world where we can have healthy dialogue and thus avoid heated arguments, or demonizing others without listening to their side of the story, and without, above all, violence. I run a newsletter that discovers our emotions and what's behind them and how we can become more productive, both in our individual lives and as a member of society. You can follow the newsletter here: The Sunglasses. You can also connect with me through Facebook or Instagram or by simply sending me an email at danielhaider@substack.com.

I am a writer who's obsessed with brain mastery and mental health. I write about sharpening your mind and becoming a more productive version of yourself. I like movies that have a time travel element.
